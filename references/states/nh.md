# New Hampshire — NHDPA

**Citation form**: New Hampshire Data Privacy Act, N.H. Rev. Stat. ch. 507-H. Effective Jan 1, 2025. Enforced by the New Hampshire Attorney General.

## Applicability

N.H. Rev. Stat. § 507-H:2. Applies to persons conducting business in NH or producing products/services targeted to NH residents AND during a calendar year:

1. Control/process PD of **at least 35,000** NH consumers (excluding solely-payment-transaction data); OR
2. Control/process PD of **at least 10,000** NH consumers AND derive over **25%** of gross revenue from sale of PD.

> Tied with DE for **lowest 35k tier**; tied with DE/MD for lowest 10k secondary tier.

Exemptions: standard.

## Consumer rights

Access, correct, delete, portability, opt-out of sale / targeted advertising / profiling. **Right to appeal** within 60 days.

## Universal opt-out signal

Required effective Jan 1, 2025.

## Sensitive data

Standard categories. **Opt-in consent required.**

## Controller duties

Privacy notice; data minimization; purpose limitation; reasonable security; non-discrimination; processor contracts; data protection assessments.

## Enforcement

AG only. **No private right of action.** Civil penalties under NH consumer protection framework. **60-day cure period** at enactment.

## NH-specific drafting considerations

1. **Lowest 25% revenue threshold** (with OR), and lowest 10k consumer trigger (with DE, MD, RI).
2. UOOM mandatory at effective date — among the earliest UOOM mandates in effect.
