# Indiana — INCDPA

**Citation form**: Indiana Consumer Data Protection Act, Ind. Code §§ 24-15 et seq. Effective Jan 1, 2026. Enforced by the Indiana Attorney General.

## Applicability

Persons conducting business in Indiana or producing products/services targeted to IN residents AND during a calendar year:

1. Control/process PD of **at least 100,000** IN consumers; OR
2. Control/process PD of **at least 25,000** IN consumers AND derive over **50%** of gross revenue from sale of PD.

Exemptions: standard (GLBA, HIPAA, FCRA, DPPA, FERPA, COPPA carve-outs; government; non-profits; higher ed; employment; B2B).

## Consumer rights

Access, correct, delete, portability, opt-out of sale / targeted advertising. **No profiling opt-out.** Right to appeal within 45 days.

## Universal opt-out signal

Required effective Jan 1, 2026.

## Sensitive data

Standard categories. **Opt-in consent required.**

## Controller duties

Privacy notice; data minimization; purpose limitation; reasonable security; non-discrimination; processor contracts; data protection assessments for high-risk activities.

## Enforcement

AG only. **No private right of action.** Civil penalty up to **$7,500 per violation**. Cure period: 30 days at enactment; verify against current AG guidance for sunset.

## IN-specific drafting considerations

1. Modeled closely on VA / CT structure.
2. **No profiling opt-out** is the notable gap relative to most modern state laws.
3. Effective date is recent — enforcement posture and AG priorities are still developing.
