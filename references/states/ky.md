# Kentucky — KCDPA

**Citation form**: Kentucky Consumer Data Protection Act, Ky. Rev. Stat. §§ 367.3611 to 367.3625. Effective Jan 1, 2026. Enforced by the Kentucky Attorney General.

## Applicability

Persons conducting business in Kentucky or producing products/services targeted to KY residents AND during a calendar year:

1. Control/process PD of **at least 100,000** KY consumers; OR
2. Control/process PD of **at least 25,000** KY consumers AND derive over **50%** of gross revenue from sale of PD.

Exemptions: standard.

## Consumer rights

Access, delete, portability, opt-out of sale / targeted advertising / profiling. **No correction right** (one of three states without one — UT, IA, KY). **Right to appeal** within 45 days.

## Universal opt-out signal

**Not required.**

## Sensitive data

Standard categories. **Opt-in consent required.**

## Controller duties

Privacy notice; data minimization; purpose limitation; reasonable security; non-discrimination; processor contracts; data protection assessments.

## Enforcement

AG only. **No private right of action.** Civil penalty up to **$7,500 per violation**. **30-day cure period.**

## KY-specific drafting considerations

1. Modeled on VA — narrow "sale" definition, 50% threshold, no UOOM.
2. **Notably no correction right** — among the narrower regimes.
3. Effective in 2026; AG enforcement posture not yet developed.
